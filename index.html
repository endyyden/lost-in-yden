BIO

Andrzej Strzemżalski (performing as Endy Yden) is a Wrocław-based composer, music producer, and multi-instrumentalist. Bridging the gap between avant-garde electronic pop and immersive media scoring, he combines a classical foundation in piano and oboe with a mastery of analog synthesis.

Theater & Film

A recognized name in the Polish performing arts scene, Andrzej creates evocative soundscapes for visionary directors. His portfolio includes the score for Jan Klata’s 2024 production of Romeo and Juliet (Capitol Musical Theatre), the award-winning feature "Supernova" (dir. Bartosz Kruhlik), and the soundtrack for the 2nd season of Netflix’s "The Green Glove Gang" (2024).

Video Games & Sound Design

As a long-time collaborator with Mass Creation and We Dig Games, he is known for a holistic approach where the boundary between music and sound design disappears. His genre-spanning work includes:

  • • Disco Samurai (2025) – Neon-soaked high-energy soundtrack.
  • • Hamster Playground (2024) – Whimsical and playful audio environment.
  • • Date Z (2024) – Atmospheric tension for narrative horror.
  • • Shing! (2020) – Rhythmic pulse with an ethnic twist.

Solo Career & Identity

Under the moniker Endy Yden, he explores cinematic melancholy and the "hauntology" of 80s synth-pop. His debut album "GTFO!" (2018) was described as a 'patchwork of sonic memories,' blending pop melodies with experimental ambitions.

Throughout his career, he has shared the stage and studio with artists such as Nils Petter Molvaer, Arve Henriksen, Golan and Hania Rani. He also co-hosts "Blok Alternatywny" on Radio RAM, shaping the musical landscape of Wrocław.
